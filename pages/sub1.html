Located in Baa Atoll, north west of Male International airport, Reethi Beach Resort is on the beautiful and lush Fonimagoodhoo Island, surrounded by a beautiful lagoon and white, sandy beach. The island is reached by a scenic 35 minutes seaplane flight.

All meals taken in the main restaurant, drinks consumed during meals limited to mineral water, soft drinks, beers and house wine. Afternoon tea, coffee and snacks, limited drinks in the main and beach bar. One free introductory dive lesson per stay and 2 half day excursions per guest per week.
